3. Power Design Recommendations

  • Provide strong decoupling capacitors near the AD8232 and MAX9814 power pins to minimize noise on the analog lines.
  • Use a solid ground plane for analog signals, potentially separating analog and digital grounds if implementing a high-fidelity board layout, joining them at a single point (star ground).
  • Ensure the main 3.3V regulator can supply enough burst current for the ESP32 Wi-Fi transmissions (minimum 500mA peak).
